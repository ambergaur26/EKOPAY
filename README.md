Ekopay: Centralized Green Credit Ecosystem
Ekopay is a centralized e-trading platform for Green Credit management, enabling users and companies to earn, buy, and sell credits to achieve carbon offsetting. The ecosystem supports sustainable growth through arbitrage tracking, Ecofunding for startups, and promotion of EcoApps. It streamlines the process for businesses to invest in eco-friendly projects, ensuring a transparent marketplace for modern sustainability goals.

 Key Features
-> e-Trading Hub: A centralized marketplace for companies to buy, sell, and list Green Credits (GCs) with full transparency.

-> Strategic Arbitrage: Real-time price tracking tools that allow companies to acquire credits at the most favorable market terms.

-> Carbon Offsetting: A streamlined pathway for industries to achieve net-zero emissions by utilizing acquired green credits.

-> Ecofunding & Startups: Integrated crowdfunding modules to support and scale up-and-coming eco-friendly ventures.

-> EcoApps Integration: A dedicated promotion space for third-party applications focused on eco-friendly initiatives and sustainable living.

-> Project Discovery: Advanced analytics to help companies discover and invest in high-impact projects like reforestation and renewable energy.

-> Incentivized Sustainability: Tools for individuals to earn credits by choosing public transport and sustainable products, turning green choices into tangible assetes.
